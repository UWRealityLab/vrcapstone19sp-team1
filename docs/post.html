Blog Post for Week 1-2 (4/1-4/12)

Posted by VR Capstone Team 1 on 4/11/19

The first week all of us spent our time going over two unity tutorials which introduced all of us to Unity basics and teach us the skills we would require to work on our project through out the quarter.

After our teams were formed at the end of first week, during second week all of us brainstormed a couple of ideas individually that we would like to work on. We got together on Tuesday after class and everyone pitched their idea to the rest of the team. After discussing all the ideas we all finalized our project that all of us thought will be a great tool to develop over the quarter. After class on thursday we all got together and refined our project idea on the comments received by the instructors and TA. After that all of us took one task and finished it before Friday noon. Matthew and Simon worked on creating the website, Esteban wrote the project proposal and Tanuj completed the blog.
